  - heading: "Overview"
    body: "StrongLight is an urban flower farm with an identity that holds two things at once: playful, dad-joke warmth on the surface, and real tenderness underneath. The system is built around a hand-drawn forget-me-not and a wordmark whose 'O' blooms into a five-petal flower, flexible enough to run from cheeky seed-packet copy to the quietest moments of grief."
  - heading: "The mark"
    body: "The wordmark sets a friendly geometric sans against a flower-in-the-O, with a hand-lettered 'urban flower farm' curving beneath. Paired with loose botanical illustration, it feels grown rather than manufactured, equal parts field and studio."

    body: "One flower emblem, endless colorways. The bloom works as a badge, a monogram, or a sticker, and the palette pulls from real petals (mustard #E6C900, deep teal #226160, tan #BD9452, charcoal #373535, and soft lavender #D8CDE5 over warm creams) so the brand can be loud or gentle as the moment needs."

  - heading: "Voice & Order Sluff"
    body: "The voice is dad jokes, satire, and warmth: 'Life is hard, so join the club.' It powers Order Sluff, a delivery concept for people in grief. A 'sluff' is an avalanche that starts from a single point, the perfect metaphor for a sudden loss. Bouquets wrapped in the funnies, cards printed on the sleeve to cry on, flowers as a way to reach the people at the hardest end of the circle and let them know they aren't alone."
